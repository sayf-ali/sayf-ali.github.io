Sayf Ali

Sayf Ali (MBA, CSM, CSPO) is a Multi-Cloud Consultant, Scrum Master, and Agile Project Manager across leading platforms such as AWS, Azure, GCP, IBM Cloud, OCI, and Salesforce. He has a 10-year proven track record of large-scale, IT implementations for Fortune 500 firms, delivering solutions for cloud adoption and migrations, data lakes/warehouses, Machine Learning, and app modernization. Sayf is meeting technical demand in industry as an Associate Full Stack Developer with a growing proficiency in JavaScript and MERN. He has supported clients in technology, pharmaceuticals, healthcare, manufacturing, finance, hospitality, and government.
